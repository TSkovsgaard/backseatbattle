Backseat Battle

A local multiplayer party game for iPhone and iPad. Pick a quick match, pass the device around, and settle the argument from the back seat.

First release includes quick lobby play, Tic-Tac-Toe, and Connect Four. No account required.

Status: coming to the App Store.
