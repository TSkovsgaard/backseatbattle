Backseat Battle Support

Need help with Backseat Battle? Send an email to support@skovsgaard.nu and include the details below.

What to include

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Backseat Battle app version
  • Short description and reproduction steps

Connection troubleshooting

  • Allow Local Network access when iOS asks for permission.
  • Keep both devices awake with Backseat Battle open.
  • Make sure both devices are nearby and on the same Wi-Fi network, or have Bluetooth enabled.
  • If a friend does not appear, close and reopen the app on both devices, then try again.

Developer support option

If you prefer GitHub, you can also open a support issue.
