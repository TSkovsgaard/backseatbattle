Backseat Battle Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026

Backseat Battle does not require account creation.

Data collection

  • We do not collect personal account data in the app.
  • We do not require cloud sign-in to play.

Local network usage

  • The app uses local network access to discover and connect nearby players for multiplayer sessions.
  • This network communication is limited to gameplay between nearby devices.

Third-party services

  • The app does not include third-party analytics SDKs in the gameplay flow described in this repository.

Contact and support

For support or privacy questions, use the official support channel: Open support issue
